Q: How can I place an order?
A: Orders can be placed directly through our website.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement.

Q: How can I check my order status?
A: Order status can be checked in your account dashboard.

Q: What happens after I place an order?
A: You will receive an order confirmation email.

Q: Can I modify my order after placing it?
A: Orders can be modified before they are shipped.

Q: What if I entered the wrong address?
A: Contact support immediately to update the address.

Q: Why was my order cancelled?
A: Orders may be cancelled due to payment issues or stock shortages.

Q: Can I order without creating an account?
A: Yes, guest checkout is available.

Q: How will I know my order shipped?
A: You will receive a shipping confirmation email.

Q: What if an item is out of stock?
A: Out-of-stock items will be indicated on the product page.
